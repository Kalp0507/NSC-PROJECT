About Us

We Are Pioneers in Scientific Innovation

Niharika Scientific Centre is the first and leading scientific store in Janakpur, dedicated to providing high-quality laboratory materials, chemicals, and educational accessories. Since its establishment in 2062 B.S. (2005 A.D.), the center has become a trusted partner for educational institutions, medical laboratories, and research facilities seeking reliable and authentic scientific supplies.

We take pride in offering a diverse range of products that cater to various scientific disciplines. Our inventory includes chemicals of different grades, suitable for academic, research, and professional use. In addition, we supply a wide selection of laboratory equipment across multiple faculties, including Mathematics, Agriculture, Pathology, and more. Whether it's a school science lab needing basic tools or an advanced medical facility requiring specialized apparatus, we ensure the timely delivery of top-quality materials to meet every unique demand.
